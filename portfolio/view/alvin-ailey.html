Alvin AIley (2010)

click to expand

Media

What it Was

The Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater is one of the most influential dance companies and schools in America. This project aimed to streamline and modernize their web presence with a content-rich site accompanied by engaging, rich media.

What I Did

As the lead developer on this project, I was responsible for architecting the system and workflow of three sites which included an event calendar, rich media uploading, donation and payment gateways, and e-mail marketing.

I also provided hands-on Drupal training for the production team in New York.

Why You Should Care

The site was nomiated as an OMMA Award finalist in the Charity/Non-Profit Organizations category.
