MasterCard Gift Finder (2009)

click to expand

Media

What it Was

The Gift Finder application suggested gifts based upon personal information entered about the person for whom you were shopping. If the person had a facebook account, you could also prepopulate the application with their data.

We also developed a "Secret Santa" Facebook application which tied into the Gift Finder, but it never saw the light of day.

What I Did

I worked on the application architecture for both applications, and floated between the two, doing everything from JavaScript, to database design, to caching strategy, to server configuration and administration.

I was also heavily involved in prototyping core functionality with Amazon & Facebook APIs.

Why You Should Care

I helped introduce the Zend framework to the technology team at HUGE and assisted the team in developing and architecting two large-scale applications in a very short timeline of two months, successfully launching (what I have been told was) MasterCard's largest online marketing campaign ever.
